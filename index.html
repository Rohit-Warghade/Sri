🏮 Our Story

Where Every Bite
Feels Like Home

Sri Prasad Snack Center was born from a heartfelt dream — to place the purest flavours of Maharashtra on your plate. For over two decades, our family has been serving freshly made, hygienic, and soulful snacks that carry the warmth of a mother's kitchen.

From our iconic Batata Vada Pav and fiery Misal Pav to the legendary Sabudana Vada — a signature dish people travel across Pune for — every item is crafted with love, fresh ingredients, and generations of recipe wisdom.

We also prepare special fasting menus for Ekadashi, Navratri & all religious occasions, and accept pre-bookings for daily lunch — freshly cooked and packed, ready by noon.
