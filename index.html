🏮 Our Story

Where Every Bite
Feels Like Home

Sri Prasad Snack Center was born from a simple dream — to bring the purest flavours of Maharashtra to your plate. For over two decades, our family has been serving freshly made, hygienic, and heartful snacks that remind every customer of their mother's kitchen.

From our iconic Batata Vada Pav and zesty Misal Pav, to the beloved Sabudana Vada — our signature dish that regulars travel across the city for — every item is crafted with love, fresh ingredients, and a generational recipe.
